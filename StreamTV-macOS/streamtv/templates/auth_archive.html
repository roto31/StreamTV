Browser Cookies Authentication (Recommended)

Export cookies from your browser to access private Archive.org collections.

info
Why is this needed?

Some Archive.org collections (like sesame-street_202306) are private and require authentication. Using browser cookies is the most reliable method.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Install a cookie export extension:
    • Chrome/Edge: Get cookies.txt LOCALLY
    • Firefox: cookies.txt
    • Safari: Export Cookies
  2. Log into Archive.org:
    • Go to archive.org/account/login
    • Sign in with your credentials
  3. Visit a private collection:
    • Navigate to the collection you want to access
    • Example: sesame-street_202306
    • Verify you can play videos
  4. Export cookies:
    • Click the cookie extension icon in your browser
    • Select "Export" or "Download as cookies.txt"
    • Save the file to your computer
  5. Upload below:
Select your exported cookies.txt file from Archive.org

What Cookies Are Used For

The cookies file contains:

  • logged-in-sig - Authentication signature
  • logged-in-user - Your username
  • session tokens - Session data

Security: Your cookies are stored locally and never shared. They work just like your browser session.

Expiration: Cookies typically last 30-90 days. When they expire, just export fresh cookies from your browser.
