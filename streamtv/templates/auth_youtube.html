About YouTube Authentication

YouTube authentication allows you to stream content from your own YouTube channel or access age-restricted content.

  • Cookies Method: Export cookies from your browser after logging into YouTube
  • OAuth Method: Direct Google OAuth (requires OAuth client setup)
  • Cookies file is stored locally and used automatically
  • You can remove authentication at any time

How to Export Cookies

  1. Install browser extension: "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" (Chrome/Edge) or "cookies.txt" (Firefox)
  2. Visit youtube.com and login
  3. Click the extension icon
  4. Select "Export" to save cookies.txt
  5. Upload the file using the form above
